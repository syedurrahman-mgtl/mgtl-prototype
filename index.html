Cost Management

Leveraging a sophisticated FEES module, we give you close command of the reconciliation of agent invoices to existing schedules and internal transaction volumes, identifying redundant account charges and automating complex processes. Through network rationalisation and the close ongoing scrutiny of account invoicing and schedule management, MYRIAD and Embus can identify redundant accounts and eradicate unnecessary fees from the outset.

Due Diligence

Powerful delta management functionality and instantaneous information exchange means a dramatic workload reduction year-on-year. Choose from products that provide either a standalone solution for Industry standard questionnaires at the lowest cost in the market, or a comprehensive platform that wraps due diligence functionality into a complete agent performance monitoring platform.

Client Onboarding

Run your onboarding function more efficiently by introducing automated processes which run in parallel, allowing you to focus on informed decision-making. Shorten time-to-market, improving speed and accuracy, boosting transparency and lowering costs through streamlined processes and services.

Account Workflow and Automation

Automation is everything - and allowing you to configure your own account management procedures is an extremely important part of what we offer. Process and control functions afford endless options for account and lifecycle management through task-led, automated workflows..

Performance Oversight

All criteria by which you measure your providers is covered by our products, whether it be issues tracked and matched to the SLA or automated RFP and scoring procedures. Cross-departmental workflows prevent fragmentation and duplication of effort, meaning that such exercises as due diligence benefit from lean process and collaboration.

Reporting

If data is not reportable, either on paper or screen, in a format that suits your purposes, then its value and accessibility are substantially reduced. The ability to summon reports that deliver intelligence to your screen or inbox, and make available to chosen colleagues, all at the touch of a button, is a vital asset.

Network Management

All MYRIAD's modules combine to form a completely comprehensive solution for the Network and Vendor Management function. Oversight of your counterparties , their reference data and documentation is complete down to the most granular element. From RFP, through due diligence, invoice management, reporting and performance, governance is absolute and will deliver substantial value in cost and productivity.
